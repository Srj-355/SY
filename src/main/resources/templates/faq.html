Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

To book a bus ticket, simply enter your departure and arrival locations, select your travel date, and choose from the available buses. Follow the prompts to complete your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with your ticket details.

We accept various payment methods including:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard)
  • Mobile Wallets (eSewa, Khalti)
  • Internet Banking
  • Cash on Board (for some operators)

You will receive your ticket confirmation via email and SMS immediately after completing your booking. You can also access your ticket details anytime by logging into your account on our website or mobile app.

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your ticket, subject to the bus operator's policies. Cancellation fees may apply depending on how close to the departure time you make the change. You can manage your booking through your account or by contacting our customer support.

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after cancellation. The amount refunded depends on the cancellation policy of the bus operator and the time of cancellation. Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for booking.

In case of bus delay or cancellation, we will notify you immediately via email and SMS. We will assist you with alternative travel arrangements on the next available bus or provide a full refund as per the operator's policy. Our customer support team is available 24/7 to help in such situations.

Each passenger is allowed one carry-on bag (max 5kg) and one checked luggage (max 15kg). Oversized or additional luggage may incur extra charges. Some operators may have different policies, which will be displayed during the booking process.

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. We never store your payment details on our servers. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered email address. You'll receive a link to reset your password. If you don't see the email, please check your spam folder.
